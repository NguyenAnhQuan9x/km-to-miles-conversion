Km To Miles Conversion

In the US, the most popular imperial unit of measurement for calculating distance between two points is the mile. The same object is measured in terms of kilometers, a metric length unit. The mathematical formula to go from kilometers to miles, or the other way around, is not too difficult.

Since kilometers are used in many countries, Americans may need to convert them into miles, a more common unit of distance in the USA.

To convert kilometers into miles, you multiply the distance value by 0.621371 because 1 km equals 0.621371 mi. The following is the formula:

miles = kilometers x 0.621371

This is the master formula for converting kilometers to miles
